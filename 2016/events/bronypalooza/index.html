BronyPalooza

25 Performances. 2 Days.

BronyPalooza is the largest concert for fandom music. With over 20 extended sets of all different varieties ranging from rock, electronic, rap, pop, and more, this two-day, fourteen-hour concert is sure to deliver the most immersive fandom musical experience to date. Are you ready to jump into the basement of the Baltimore Convention Center and raise the roof even higher this year?

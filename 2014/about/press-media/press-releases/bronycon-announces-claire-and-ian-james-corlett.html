BronyCon Announces Claire and Ian James Corlett

 May. 30, 2014

(Baltimore, Md.)—We are excited to announce that the father-daughter duo Claire and Ian James Corlett will be coming to BronyCon this Summer!

Claire is mostly known to fans as the spoken voice of Sweetie Belle in “My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic,” whom Claire also picked up the singing role for in MLP’s fourth season. Additional voice roles for Claire include Tiny on “Dinosaur Train,” Stacy in “Barbie and Her Sisters in a Pony Tale,” Chloe in “Chloe’s Closet,” Michelle in “3-2-1 Penguins,” Sally in “Peanuts Motion Comics” and her film debut as Frank in “Smart Cookies.”

Ian, Claire’s father, landed his first voice role with MLP in the fourth season where he lent his talent to the character Silver Shill. Outside of MLP, Ian has provided voice-overs for many other animated-TV series such as “Dragon Tales,” “Care Bears” and “Johnny Test.” He also has done extensive promo work for national radio and TV, including the networks and cable stations PBS, Fox Family and Canada’s Family Channel.

To learn more about guests appearing at BronyCon, visit the guest page. Don’t be the last to hear these announcements and make sure you are following BronyCon on Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, and Google+.

About BronyCon

Based in Baltimore, Md., BronyCon is the premiere convention for My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic fans of all ages. Last year’s event drew more than 8,400 attendees from all seven continents.This year’s event, scheduled for Aug. 1-3, 2014 at the Baltimore Convention Center, is expected to trump that figure. The convention features special guests, diverse programming, and a multi-night music festival. For more information, check out bronycon.org.

Be on the lookout for additional exciting announcements in the coming weeks.

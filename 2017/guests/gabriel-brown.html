Gabriel Brown

← More BronyCon 2017 Guests

Gabriel Brown (aka Black Gryph0n) is a 27 year old singer, actor, musician, arranger, song-writer, animator and voiceover artist who recently voiced lead roles on Geronimo Stilton and for Becky the Loon in Pixar’s Finding Dory. At the age of 18, Gabe joined the US Navy and was training as a SEAL when he broke his femur in a sky diving accident. He switched to the Navy’s music department and spent three years touring the Eastern Hemisphere and singing in 12 languages as lead singer for the 7th Fleet Band Orient Express. Now separated with an honourable discharge, Gabe continues to sing on stage and in the studio. Notable credits include soundtrack vocals for My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic (Season 5, Equestria Girls and Songs of Harmony CD), Littlest Pet Shop, the theme song lead vocals for Pac-Man & the Ghostly Adventures, a hit CD, Immortal, with his brother Baasik and several collaborative albums with Michelle Creber, including their latest Getting Stronger. Gabe is also a talented animator (known for his licious series), photographer and graphic designer.

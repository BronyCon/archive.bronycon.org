Michelle Creber and Gabriel Brown Get Ready to Jam at BronyCon

 Apr. 26, 2018

(BALTIMORE)—It's time to lower the windows and crank up the music as we race down the highway towards our July 27–29 event in Baltimore. The next Guests of Honor can help with that, as we welcome back Michelle Creber and Gabriel Brown to their third straight year at BronyCon!

Creber, a multi-talented performer with past roles as an actor, singer, dancer, musician, songwriter and voice-over artist, is known internationally as the voice of Apple Bloom in “My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic.” Earlier in the series run, she also performed as the singing voice of Sweetie Belle. Her other on-screen credits include “Supernatural,” Disney’s “Search for Santa Paws,” Hallmark’s “Smart Cookies” and The Hub’s “Haunting Hour.”

Brown, a singer, actor, voice actor, songwriter and animator, is perhaps more well known to “MLP” fans as Black Gryph0n. Gabe joined the US Navy in special operations at the age of 18, before touring the Eastern Hemisphere as the lead singer of the 7th Fleet Band “Orient Express.” Since leaving the military, he has continued to pursue a career in entertainment. His vocal credits include various “MLP” songs, as well as the lead vocals for “Pac-Man & the Ghostly Adventures” theme.

Together, Creber and Brown have released several collaborative albums and have worked together on projects such as concerts, tours, original songs, song covers, vlogs, and a comedy series called “Kristoff & Schloff” among other things.

Those interested in learning more about Creber and Brown, along with the other amazing guests appearing at BronyCon this year, can visit the guest page on our website.

To register for this event

To register for this event, visit BronyCon’s registration page. Badges for BronyCon will cost more at the door, so don’t wait! Also make sure you are following BronyCon on Facebook, Twitter, and Telegram to be the first to hear our latest announcements.

About BronyCon

Based in Baltimore, Md., the tenth iteration of BronyCon is scheduled for July 27-29, 2018 at the Baltimore Convention Center. The convention features special guests, diverse programming, events, and a multi-night music festival featuring 14 hours of live music from some of the greatest fandom musicians. For more information, check out bronycon.org.

Autograph Core Hours

Autograph services are cash only and purchased directly at each guest’s table. See the 2018 Autograph Policies for more information.

Guests of honor will be signing autographs whenever they are not at a panel, scheduled event, or on break. Ad hoc signing sessions are in addition to the core hours listed below and will be posted in the autographs area at the convention.

Autograph and photograph prices are set directly by the guest and their agent and may be subject to change over the weekend. Pricing information will be available at the convention and shared in advance if it is known.

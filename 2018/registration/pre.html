Other Badge Types

Enjoy a single day at the convention!

Friday: $40; Saturday: $40; Sunday: $30

Register Single Day

Youth

With the purchase of any adult 3-day badge, discounted youth passes are available for fans 13-and-under at $40. 5-and-under get in free.

Service Member 3-Day

Special 3-Day rate for fire, police, military, and EMS plus dependents with valid ID at pickup.

Register Service Member 3-Day

Senior 3-Day

Senior discounted 3-Day badge for those 65 and over with valid ID.
